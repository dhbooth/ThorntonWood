Governance

Thornton Wood’s condominium documents consist of the Declaration, the Condominium Plat, and the By-Laws. These documents are all duly recorded in the Land Records of Baltimore County.


Declaration

Declaration
The Declaration is a legal document under the Maryland Real Property Act that created the Condominium Regime known as "Thornton Wood Condominium." It contains basic definitions of the Condominium as well as restrictions and limitations of its use and operation. Available below, this document has not been amended since its creation in 1974.




Bylaws

By-Laws
The By-Laws are provided for under the Maryland Condominium Law and define the Condominium Association’s governance and operation. The powers delegated to the Board of Directors by the Council of Unit Owners are enumerated and defined within. A copy of the By-Laws and a history of amendments is provided below.




Contact

Board Minutes
All Unit Owners are entitled to copies of the approved minutes from the meetings of the Thornton Wood Condominium Board of Directors and the Council of Unit Owners. Please direct copy requests to the Property Manager.

Regs

Rules and Regulations
As provided for in the By-Laws and approved by the Board of Directors and the Council of Unit Owners, the Rules and Regulations further detail key aspects of the Condominium’s operations, including such matters as architectural control, use restrictions, insurance, maintenance and repair, approval protocols, and enforcement. A copy is provided below.
